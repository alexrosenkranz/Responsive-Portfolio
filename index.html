About Me

I'm Alex Rosenkranz, a web developer by day and music nut by night. I grew up by Asbury Park, so music and beach culture have always played a huge role in shaping my life. I graduated Rutgers University in 2009 with a B.A. in Information Technology and have been a web developer (and sometimes graphic designer) ever since.

I'm incredibly interested in new technology in really any field as long as I find it to have a positive impact on society, especially pertaining to the discovery & curation of music. When I'm not working on web sites and applications, you can usually find me enjoying a good drink with friends or at a concert.

If you want to know more about me, why don't you look to your right and click on one of those fancy icons?
